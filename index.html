ABOUT US

Combination Industries pioneered production in Nigeria’s Premier Export Processing Zone – the Calabar Export Processing Zone – under NEPZA in May 1999. With the able guidance of their principal’s company Geekay International Exports, USA, they introduced NikSnacks, a new brand of top-quality snack products (starting with Cheese Balls and Cheezy Tastee Snacks) into Nigeria and the West African continent. Today, the products are very well-known and well-distributed in the Nigerian and West African community.

In the past few years Niksnacks have been exported to Nigeria, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Liberia, Cameroon, Sudan, Sao Tome & Principe, Togo, Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger and UK The place that the Company holds in the Nigerian economy was underlined when it was privileged to be visited by the several state heads including country’s first President, Chief Olusegun Obasanjo.
